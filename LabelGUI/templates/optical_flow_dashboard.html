What This Shows

This page uses the saved optical-flow results from flow_v1. The speeds shown here are normalized image-motion values, not real-world meters per second. They are useful for comparing motion patterns between takeoff, landing, minor crash, and severe crash clips.
ROI Available Rate: how often a usable drone-focused region was available.
Both-Detected Rate: how often the drone was detected in both consecutive frames.
Detected-Center Speed: movement of the detected drone center over time.
Optical-Flow Speed: pixel motion inside the drone-focused region.
